# Research

UTKa DataLab’s research is organized around two themes, together spanning five active projects across PhD and EngD tracks.

## AI with Authoritative Data

We investigate how Large Language Models and AI systems can be made more reliable and trustworthy when grounded in authoritative knowledge graphs. This addresses core challenges in government data applications, including hallucinations and limited explainability in AI-generated outputs. A key example of such a government knowledge graph is the Kadaster Knowledge Graph ([Ronzhin et al. 2019](#ref-ronzhin2019kadaster)).

## Trusted Federative Data Infrastructures

We design secure data-sharing architectures that respect privacy and data sovereignty. Our work centers on **Virtual Knowledge Graph (VKG)** infrastructures that enable efficient cross-system querying without requiring data replication or centralization.
