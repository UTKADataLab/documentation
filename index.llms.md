# UTKa DataLab

Advancing Federated Data Research

UTKa DataLab is a joint research initiative between [Kadaster](https://www.kadaster.nl) and the [University of Twente](https://www.utwente.nl), based in Apeldoorn. We develop methods to extract, combine, and analyze information from distributed data sources, with a strong commitment to ethical conduct, scientific integrity, and open science.

## Research Themes

- [AI with Authoritative Data](research.llms.md#ai-with-authoritative-data) — grounding LLMs and AI systems in authoritative knowledge graphs
- [Trusted Federative Data Infrastructures](research.llms.md#trusted-federative-data-infrastructures) — secure, privacy-respecting architectures for cross-system data sharing
